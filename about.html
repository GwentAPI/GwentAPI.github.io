RESTful API for GWENT®: The Witcher Card Game

What is GwentAPI?

GwentAPI is a RESTful API dedicated to provide information regarding GWENT. You may use its interface to consume information on cards, factions, patches, etc.

Why would I use GwentAPI?

GwentAPI provides its database to the community. If you want to start your own community, app, website, or wiki, that would require you to input a significant amount of data in your own database. We can alleviate that process by giving you the data. All that is left for you is to process it.

What is v0?

v0 is the public sneak peak of GwentAPI. GWENT is in early stage of developement and all data in our database is currently based on the E3 2016 build. Once the closed beta starts in September not only we will have a lot more data, the data will be a closer representation of the final game. This will allow us to finalize our model for v1. Please note that we will cease to serve v0 after the GWENT beta. We will give an advance notice so that you may have enought time to support v1 on your end. In the future we will continue to serve previous versions.

Are there any conditions to use the API?

We ask you to cache the results to keep traffic as low as possible on our end. We also recommand that you don't hardcode URLs but we will not go out of our way to change resources URL.

There is something wrong with your service/API

We aim at improving at every opportunity. You can contribute to the project on GitHub. You can also reach us on twitter @GwentAPI

How did you build it?

  • The webserver is Nginx, we use it as our cache server and reverse proxy
  • The application is written in Go with the help of the Goa framework
  • Our database is MySQL
  • DigitalOcean is our host
